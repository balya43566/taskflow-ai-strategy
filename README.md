# TaskFlow AI Competitive Response Strategy

Competitive intelligence research and executive response strategy for TaskFlow leadership, prepared in response to competitor AI chat feature launches.

## Key Findings

- **All 5 major competitors** now have some form of AI chat
- **ClickUp Brain** is the #1 threat — most mature "chat with your work" implementation on the market
- **30–90 days** before AI-driven churn risk peaks at contract renewals
- **Pricing opportunity**: every competitor charges $15–28/user/month extra for AI — TaskFlow can undercut
